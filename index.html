Even though the Atlantic Hurricane Season only officially began on June 1, Dominion Energy has been preparing for months. "Summer storms can be unpredictable so planning ahead and being ready is key," explains David Vanderbloemen, director of the Electric Distribution Operations Center. "Part of our preparation includes using new technologies to restore power faster if it does go out," he explains. But there's a lot that you, the customer, can do to stay safe, too.
